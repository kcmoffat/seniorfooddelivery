FAQ

Can I really get food delivered from ANY restaurant?

Yes! We even deliver from restaurants that don't offer in-house delivery to customers. All we require is that the restaurant offers take-out - if so, we can pick up your items and deliver them to your door. It's like magic! Our independent delivery network, powered by Postmates, makes this possible.


Do I have to cook the meals?

No, we deliver hot and ready meals from your favorite restaurants.


I'm a caretaker. Does my relative need internet access or a smartphone to use Magic Meals?

No! We created Magic Meals so that seniors and homebound individuals have access to the same food delivery conveniences available to smartphone users, without needing a smartphone or internet access. Call us anytime to set up a delivery schedule!


When do my meals arrive?

Whenever you'd like! You specify the day(s) and time(s) for delivery.


Is there a setup fee?

No! You can start a meal plan at any time with no setup fee.


Can I cancel my meal plan at any time?

Of course! You can upgrade, change, or cancel a recurring delivery at any time. Just give us a call!


How do I pay for Magic Meals?

We accept most major credit cards. Deliveres are prepaid every month for the next month of deliveries. Call us to get started!
